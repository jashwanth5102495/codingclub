Welcome to Team Anveshak

Exploring,Innovating, and Evolving through Technology

Team Anveshak is the official technical team of Akash Global College of Management and Science, launched on April 11th, 2025, with a mission to inspire innovation and empower students through technology-driven learning. Our goal is to create a vibrant platform where creativity meets technology — fostering skills, collaboration, and practical knowledge among students. From technical workshops and certification programs to project showcases and hackathons, Team Anveshak serves as a hub for all tech enthusiasts eager to explore the digital world. We believe in learning by doing, encouraging every member to take part in hands-on activities, technical events, and collaborative projects that shape future-ready professionals.

Join us as we explore, innovate, and lead the way into the world of technology!

Who We Are

Team Anveshak is a vibrant community of innovators, learners, and tech enthusiasts at Akash Global College of Management and Science. Formed with the vision of creating a space where ideas come to life, the team strives to inspire curiosity, encourage experimentation, and empower students to push the boundaries of technology.

Our Purpose We aim to bridge the gap between theory and practice by encouraging students to apply their knowledge through real-world technical experiences. Team Anveshak focuses on nurturing technical skills, leadership qualities, and teamwork among its members.


Our Impact Through our initiatives, students gain exposure to emerging technologies, collaborate on meaningful projects, and develop a mindset ready for the digital future. We take pride in fostering a community that learns, grows, and leads together.

UPCOMMING EVENTS

🔹 Upcoming Hackathon – Quiz Edition


Team Anveshak is thrilled to announce its next exciting initiative — a hackathon in the form of a quiz, scheduled for 29th October 2025. Unlike traditional coding challenges, this event will test students theoretical knowledge, problem-solving skills, and understanding of technical concepts in a fast-paced, competitive environment.

The quiz aims to identify and nurture talent, encouraging students to think critically, respond quickly, and apply their knowledge creatively. From all participants, 20 outstanding students will be selected to form Team Sankalp, who will represent their skills and teamwork in this unique challenge.

This event is designed to be more than just a competition. It is a platform for learning, collaboration, and intellectual growth, inspiring participants to push their limits and explore the depth of their technical understanding. With Team Anveshak hosting, students can look forward to a well-organized, engaging, and memorable experience that celebrates curiosity, knowledge, and the spirit of innovation. Get ready to think, compete, and excel!
